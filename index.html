Indivisible Somerville

Indivisible Somerville opposes the Trump agenda. We began as an offshoot of the Somerville Democrats, and have recently morphed into a chapter of Indivisible, organizing folks in Somerville, Massachusetts and the surrounding communinities. We strive to be inclusive and collaborative.
